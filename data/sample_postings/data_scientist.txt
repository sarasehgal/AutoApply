Data Scientist — Healthcare Analytics

We're building analytics tools for hospital systems to reduce patient readmission rates. We're
looking for a Data Scientist to join our small, senior team.

What you'll do:
- Build statistical and machine learning models to predict patient readmission risk.
- Design and run experiments (A/B tests, causal inference) to evaluate intervention programs.
- Communicate findings clearly to clinical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Partner with data engineering to ensure clean, well-documented data pipelines.

Required qualifications:
- Master's or PhD in Statistics, Biostatistics, Computer Science, or a related quantitative field.
- 3+ years of experience applying statistical modeling or machine learning to real-world problems.
- Strong SQL skills and experience with R or Python for statistical analysis.
- Experience with causal inference or clinical/healthcare data is a strong plus.

Preferred qualifications:
- Experience with survival analysis or longitudinal patient data.
- Familiarity with HIPAA-compliant data handling practices.
- Experience presenting to non-technical clinical stakeholders.

Seniority: Mid-to-Senior
