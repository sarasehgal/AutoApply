Platform Engineer — Cloudline

Cloudline runs infra for a few hundred mid-size SaaS companies. Looking for a Platform Engineer
to help scale our deployment tooling and internal developer platform.

What you'll do:
- Build and maintain Kubernetes-based deployment pipelines used by ~50 internal engineers.
- Improve observability (logs, metrics, tracing) across our services.
- Automate infra provisioning with Terraform and reduce manual ops work.
- On-call rotation for platform-level incidents.

Required:
- 3+ years working with Kubernetes and Docker in production.
- Strong Python or Go skills for tooling/automation.
- Experience with AWS (EC2, IAM, VPC basics).
- Comfortable writing and maintaining CI/CD pipelines.

Nice to have:
- Terraform or other infra-as-code experience.
- Experience with async Python for internal tooling.
- Experience mentoring engineers on infra best practices.

Seniority: Mid-level (3-5 years)
