Senior Machine Learning Engineer — Acme Recommendations

Acme Recommendations builds personalization infrastructure for e-commerce platforms. We're
hiring a Senior ML Engineer to own the core recommendation system that serves millions of
users daily.

What you'll do:
- Design, build, and ship machine learning models that power product recommendations at scale.
- Own the full lifecycle: data pipeline, training, evaluation, deployment, and monitoring.
- Partner with product and data science to translate business goals into ML problems.
- Improve retrieval-augmented systems that ground model outputs in real product/catalog data.
- Mentor junior engineers and help set technical direction for the ML platform team.

Required qualifications:
- 4+ years of experience building and deploying machine learning systems in production.
- Strong Python skills; hands-on experience with PyTorch or a comparable deep learning framework.
- Experience with recommendation systems, ranking, or personalization.
- Experience with cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and containerized deployments (Docker/Kubernetes).

Preferred qualifications:
- Experience with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), vector databases, or LLM-based systems.
- Experience with LangChain or similar orchestration frameworks.
- Experience mentoring or leading a small team of engineers.

Seniority: Senior (4+ years experience)
